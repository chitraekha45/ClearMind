About ClearMind

Our story and our philosophy.

Meet Our Founder

Dipankar

Dipankar is the founder of ClearMind Coaching & Counselling. With a passion for guiding individuals towards inner peace and understanding, Dipankar has created a practice that emphasizes empathy, wisdom, and a holistic approach to mental well-being.

His vision is to provide a safe and supportive space where clients can explore their challenges and discover their strengths, empowering them to lead fulfilling lives.

Our Story

ClearMind was born from the belief that true healing comes not from quick solutions but from understanding the deeper stories beneath the surface.

Think of therapy with Clear Mind as a deep dive into your inner workings, not with the intensity of a surgical strike, but with the calm of a meditation session. Here, we’re not just talking about problems but understanding them in the context of your whole life. It’s therapy, yes, but it feels more like a series of conversations with a wise friend who helps you untangle the knots, not by pulling harder but by showing you how they can be gently undone.

Dr. Dipankar Khanna created ClearMind to redefine therapy — making it feel less like a clinical session and more like a thoughtful conversation with someone who truly understands you. Having trained internationally and studied diverse schools of thought in psychology, Dr. Khanna distilled his approach into something simple yet profound: guiding individuals toward clarity by combining deep listening with scientific insight.

Our Logo: The Eternal Knot

At the heart of the ClearMind logo lies the Eternal Knot of Wisdom - a timeless symbol representing interconnectedness, balance, and the cyclical nature of life.

Just like the Eternal Knot has no beginning or end, the mind's journey toward healing is continuous. ClearMind helps clients untangle emotional complexities - not by forcing solutions, but by guiding them to see the deeper patterns at play.
